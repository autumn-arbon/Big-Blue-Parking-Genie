About Big Blue's Parking Genie

And The Team Behind It

What Is Big Blue's Parking Genie?

You know how home owners near universities often charge people to park in their driveways for football games? Most of the time, if you want to park in a local's driveway, you have to show up extra early or park extra far away. Big Blue's Parking Genie aims to resolve this issue with a parking reservation app for Utah State University Events. Here, you can either buy and reserve a parking spot ahead of time or list your own parking spots. Our goal is to connect drivers to parking lots (or driveways) before the event. Doing so saves time, prevents stress, and decreases wandering traffic.

Who Are The Creators?

We are a team goes by the name The Djinns. The four of us (Joel Christensen, Autumn Arbon, Lexy Simmons, and Palani Johnson) have created this for the purpose of fulfilling a school project and to fulfill a need in the community. We are all working toward Bachelor Degrees in Computer Science. We are motivated by the desire to recieve that degree and by our mascot, Palani's cat.
